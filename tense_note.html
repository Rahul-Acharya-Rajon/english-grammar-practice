Tense

ইংরেজিতে মোট ১২টা Tense আছে। এগুলোকে ৩ ভাগে ভাগ করা হয়:

Tense-এর মূল ৩ প্রকার:

  1. Present Tense
  2. Past Tense
  3. Future Tense

প্রত্যেকটার আবার ৪টা রূপ:

  • Simple
  • Continuous
  • Perfect
  • Perfect Continuous

১. Present Tense

1. Present Simple Tense

Structure: Subject + Verb (base form) + s/es (3rd person singular)

  • Affirmative: I eat / He eats
  • Negative: I do not eat / He does not eat
  • Question: Do you eat? / Does he eat?

ব্যবহার:

  • সাধারণ সত্য (Universal truth)
  • অভ্যাস (Habits)
  • নিয়মিত কাজ
  • Schedule/Program

উদাহরণ:

  • The sun rises in the east.
  • I go to school every day.
  • She loves music.

Signal words: always, usually, often, sometimes, never, every day, etc.

2. Present Continuous Tense

Structure: Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing

  • Affirmative: I am eating
  • Negative: I am not eating
  • Question: Am I eating?

ব্যবহার:

  • এখন চলছে এমন কাজ
  • ভবিষ্যতে নির্দিষ্ট পরিকল্পনা
  • অস্থায়ী পরিস্থিতি

উদাহরণ:

  • I am writing a letter now.
  • They are coming tomorrow.
  • He is living in Dhaka these days.

Signal words: now, right now, at this moment, at present, etc.

3. Present Perfect Tense

Structure: Subject + have/has + V3 (Past Participle)

  • Affirmative: I have eaten
  • Negative: I have not eaten
  • Question: Have you eaten?

ব্যবহার:

  • অতীতে শুরু হয়ে বর্তমানে প্রভাব আছে
  • অভিজ্ঞতা (Experience)
  • এখনো শেষ হয়নি এমন সময়ে

উদাহরণ:

  • I have just finished my work.
  • She has visited Cox’s Bazar twice.
  • They have lived here for 5 years.

Signal words: just, already, yet, ever, never, for, since, recently, lately.

4. Present Perfect Continuous Tense

Structure: Subject + have/has + been + Verb-ing

ব্যবহার:

  • অতীতে শুরু হয়ে এখনো চলছে
  • জোর দেওয়া হয় কতক্ষণ ধরে চলছে তার উপর

উদাহরণ:

  • It has been raining since morning.
  • I have been learning English for 2 years.
  • She has been waiting for you for 3 hours.

২. Past Tense

5. Past Simple Tense

Structure: Subject + V2 (Past form)

ব্যবহার:

  • অতীতে সম্পূর্ণ শেষ হয়ে যাওয়া কাজ
  • গল্পের ঘটনা

উদাহরণ:

  • I went to Sylhet yesterday.
  • He bought a new phone last week.
  • They played football.

Signal words: yesterday, last week, ago, in 2020, etc.

6. Past Continuous Tense

Structure: Subject + was/were + Verb-ing

ব্যবহার:

  • অতীতে কোনো সময়ে চলছিল এমন কাজ
  • দুটো কাজ একসাথে চলছিল (while/when)

উদাহরণ:

  • I was sleeping when you called.
  • They were playing while it was raining.

7. Past Perfect Tense

Structure: Subject + had + V3

ব্যবহার:

  • অতীতের আগের অতীত (Past before Past)

উদাহরণ:

  • I had eaten before he came.
  • She had left when I reached the station.

Signal words: before, after, when, by the time, already, just.

8. Past Perfect Continuous Tense

Structure: Subject + had + been + Verb-ing

উদাহরণ:

  • I had been studying for 5 hours before the power went out.
  • They had been waiting for the bus for 2 hours.

৩. Future Tense

9. Future Simple Tense

Structure: Subject + will/shall + Verb (base)

ব্যবহার:

  • ভবিষ্যতে করা কাজ (সিদ্ধান্ত, প্রতিশ্রুতি, ভবিষ্যদ্বাণী)

উদাহরণ:

  • I will call you tomorrow.
  • It will rain today.

10. Future Continuous Tense

Structure: Subject + will be + Verb-ing

উদাহরণ:

  • I will be waiting for you at 5 PM.
  • They will be playing cricket at this time tomorrow.

11. Future Perfect Tense

Structure: Subject + will have + V3

উদাহরণ:

  • I will have finished the work by 10 PM.
  • She will have completed her degree by 2027.

12. Future Perfect Continuous Tense

Structure: Subject + will have been + Verb-ing

উদাহরণ:

  • By December, I will have been working here for 3 years.
  • Next year, they will have been living in London for 10 years.

সারাংশ টেবিল (মুখস্থ করার জন্য)

Tense Form সংক্ষিপ্ত ব্যবহার
Present Simple V1 / V1+s অভ্যাস, সত্য
Present Continuous am/is/are + V-ing এখন চলছে
Present Perfect have/has + V3 অতীত-বর্তমান সংযোগ
Present Perfect Cont. have/has been + V-ing কতক্ষণ ধরে চলছে
Past Simple V2 অতীতে শেষ
Past Continuous was/were + V-ing অতীতে চলছিল
Past Perfect had + V3 অতীতের আগে
Past Perfect Cont. had been + V-ing অতীতে কতক্ষণ চলছিল
Future Simple will + V1 ভবিষ্যতে করব
Future Continuous will be + V-ing ভবিষ্যতে চলবে
Future Perfect will have + V3 ভবিষ্যতে শেষ হয়ে যাবে
Future Perfect Cont. will have been + V-ing ভবিষ্যতে কতক্ষণ চলবে

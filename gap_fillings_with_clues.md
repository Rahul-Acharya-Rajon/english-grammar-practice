### **১. Right Form of Verbs (সবচেয়ে বেশি আসে)**

**নিয়মসমূহ:**

1. **Subject-verb agreement** দেখো (Singular/Plural)
2. **Tense** বুঝো (বাক্যের অর্থ অনুসারে)
3. **Voice** (Active/Passive)
4. **Modal verb** থাকলে base form ব্যবহার করো
5. **Non-finite verb** (to + v1, v1+ing, v3)

**গুরুত্বপূর্ণ ক্লুস ও উদাহরণ:**

| ক্লু                        | নিয়ম                | উদাহরণ                                            |
| ------------------------- | ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------ |
| **Every day / Usually**   | Present Indefinite | He (go) → **goes**                               |
| **Now / At this moment**  | Present Continuous | She (read) → **is reading**                      |
| **Already / Just / Yet**  | Present Perfect    | They (finish) → **have finished**                |
| **Yesterday / Last year** | Past Indefinite    | I (see) → **saw**                                |
| **While / When (দুটো ঘটনা)** | Past Continuous    | I (sleep) when he came → **was sleeping**        |
| **By + সময়**              | Perfect Tense      | By 2025, he (complete) → **will have completed** |
| **be + being + v3**       | Passive Continuous | The road (repair) → **is being repaired**        |
| **to + verb**             | Infinitive         | He wants (go) → **to go**                        |
| **after/before + v-ing**  | Gerund             | After (eat) → **eating**                         |

**উদাহরণ অনুশীলন:**
The teacher (a) — (be) very honest. He always (b) — (speak) the truth. Yesterday he (c) — (catch) a student copying. The student (d) — (ask) to leave the class.

**উত্তর:** (a) is (b) speaks (c) caught (d) was asked



### **২. Parts of Speech / Word Formation (Clues দিয়ে শব্দ পরিবর্তন)**

এখানে একটি শব্দ দিয়ে তার সঠিক রূপ (noun, verb, adjective, adverb) বানাতে হয়।

**নিয়ম ও সাধারণ সাফিক্স (Suffix):**

- **Noun:** ment, tion, sion, ness, ity, er, or, ist, ism
- **Adjective:** ful, less, able, ible, ous, ive, al, ic, y
- **Adverb:** ly, wise, ward
- **Verb:** en, ify, ize, ate

**উদাহরণ টেবিল:**

| শব্দ     | Noun      | Adjective   | Adverb        | Verb       |
| ------- | --------- | ----------- | ------------- | ---------- |
| Beauty  | beauty    | beautiful   | beautifully   | beautify   |
| Success | success   | successful  | successfully  | succeed    |
| Educate | education | educational | educationally | educate    |
| Happy   | happiness | happy       | happily       | —          |
| Strong  | strength  | strong      | strongly      | strengthen |

**উদাহরণ অনুশীলন:**

Fill in the gaps with the correct form of the words in brackets:

Bangladesh is an (a) — (agriculture) country. Most people are (b) — (literacy). We should work (c) — (hard) to develop the country. The (d) — (govern) is trying to solve this problem.

**উত্তর:**  
(a) agricultural  
(b) illiterate  
(c) hard  
(d) government



### **সাধারণ টিপস (খুব গুরুত্বপূর্ণ)**

1. **পুরো প্যাসেজ** একবার পড়ে নাও — মূল অর্থ বুঝে নাও।
2. **প্রত্যেক ফাঁকা জায়গার আগে-পরের শব্দ** দেখো।
3. **Article (a, an, the)** দেখে Noun বুঝো।
4. **Preposition (in, on, to, for)** এর পর সাধারণত Noun/gerund আসে।
5. **Adjective** এর আগে সাধারণত Adverb আসে।
6. **be verb (is/am/are/was/were)** এর পর Adjective বা v3 আসতে পারে।
7. Negative শব্দ (not, never, no) থাকলে অর্থ উল্টো করে নাও (happy → unhappy)।



**একটি পুরো উদাহরণ (HSC স্ট্যান্ডার্ড):**

**Fill in the blanks with appropriate form of the words given in the brackets:**

Honesty is a great (a) — (virtuous). An honest man is (b) — (respect) by all. He leads a (c) — (peace) life. His (d) — (wise) and (e) — (sincere) make him (f) — (love) to everyone.

**উত্তর:**  
(a) virtue  
(b) respected  
(c) peaceful  
(d) wisdom  
(e) sincerity  
(f) lovable
